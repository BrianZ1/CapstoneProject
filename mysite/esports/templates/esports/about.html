Welcome to my Website

This is the temporary home of my capstone project. While far from perfect, I wanted to show off what I have been working on for the past semester. Any feedback on the concept, design, or functionality is appreciated.

The goal of this project is to create a tool that will allow people to get information about specific E-Sports players. Users will be able to search for either a player or an event from a list of games. A summary will then be provided based on the articles this program finds. I believe that the deeper your connection is with the players the more invested you will be when watching those players. Knowing something about players is the first step in building those connection.
